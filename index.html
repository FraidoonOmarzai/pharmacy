NHS Services

Funded NHS Pharmacy Care

As an NHS-contracted pharmacy, Hathaway Pharmacy provides a comprehensive range of clinically commissioned services — all free for eligible patients. Our team works closely with local GP surgeries, PCNs, and ICBs to deliver joined-up care in your community.

"We're more than a dispensary. From medication optimisation to preventive health screening, we're an accessible clinical resource for every patient we serve."
💊

Repeat & Electronic Dispensing (EPS)

Collect your regular medicines hassle-free. We accept NHS electronic prescriptions directly — no paper slip needed.

💉

Seasonal Flu & COVID Vaccination

Annual influenza and COVID booster jabs for eligible NHS patients — walk-in, no GP appointment needed.

🩺

Hypertension Case-Finding Service

Free ambulatory blood pressure monitoring and referral pathway for patients identified as hypertensive.

🚭

NHS Stop Smoking Programme

Behavioural support combined with evidence-based NRT and varenicline to help you quit for good.

📦

Monitored Dosage Systems (MDS)

Weekly Dosette or blister-packed medicines for patients who need extra help managing complex medication regimens.

♻️

Medication Disposal Service

Safe, environmentally responsible disposal of unwanted or out-of-date medicines — always free, no questions asked.

❤️

Structured Medication Review (SMR)

In-depth clinical review of your medicines with a pharmacist prescriber to optimise therapy and minimise side-effects.

🔬

Drug Treatment & Supervised Consumption

Supervised methadone and buprenorphine dispensing as part of NHS community drug treatment programmes.

🛡️

Emergency Hormonal Contraception (EHC)

Same-day, confidential supply of the morning-after pill (Levonorgestrel or ellaOne) without a GP visit.

✈️

Travel Health & Immunisation Clinic

Destination-specific vaccines (Typhoid, Hep A/B, Yellow Fever), malaria prophylaxis, and fit-to-fly certificates.

⚖️

GLP-1 Weight Management (Mounjaro / Wegovy)

Clinically supervised weight-loss injection programmes with pharmacist-led monitoring and lifestyle coaching.

💪

Men's Health & ED Clinic

Private, judgement-free consultation for erectile dysfunction — prescription issued and dispensed in a single visit.

💇

Androgenetic Alopecia (Hair Loss)

Evidence-based treatments including Finasteride and Minoxidil with personalised pharmacist assessment.

📅

Menstrual Cycle Management (Period Delay)

Prescription-strength Norethisterone supplied discreetly to postpone menstruation for holidays or special events.

📝

Private Prescription Dispensing

Fast, discreet dispensing of private prescriptions from any registered UK prescriber — no NHS wait times.

💉

Pneumococcal & Private Flu Vaccination

Private immunisation for non-NHS-eligible patients, corporate clients, and those wanting early-season protection.

Pharmacy First

Get Treated Today — No GP Needed

The NHS Pharmacy First scheme empowers our pharmacists to clinically assess, diagnose, and supply prescription-only medicines for seven common conditions — reducing demand on GP surgeries and getting you better, faster.

Our pharmacists are independent prescribers — fully qualified to assess your symptoms and issue a clinical treatment plan under the Pharmacy First framework, at no cost to eligible NHS patients.
